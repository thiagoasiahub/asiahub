Dropshipping

A form of retail business wherein the seller accepts customer orders but does not keep goods sold in stock.

Step 1

Customer places order pays Asiahub retail price ($200).

Step 2

Asiahub forward order to supplier and pay wholesale ($150).

Step 3

Supplier ships the product directly to costumer.

Step 4

Asiahub Keep $50 Profit.

Step 5

Customer is assisted by our kick-ass team.

Step 6

Product arrive to customer and everybody is happy :)
